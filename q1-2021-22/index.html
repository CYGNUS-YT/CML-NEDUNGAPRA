Q1 2021-22

പ്രവർത്തന വർഷാരംഭം (The New Year of Activity)  June 1, 2021

The young missionaries of the Nedungapra Sakha enthusiastically started the new year of activity with the desire to give good to others and eagerness to follow in the footsteps of Christ and to become the gospel.

Executive Committee  June 2, 2021 

The Executive Committee, headed by Branch President, Amalekh Biju, discussed the plans for the new year online and decided to move forward with vigor.

World Environment Day  June 5, 2021 

As part of the Environment Day celebrations, the children were encouraged to plant tree saplings in their homes to make them aware of the importance of this day and the children took part in the tree planting publicity campaign.

Diocesan Council and Annual Inauguration of the Year  June 6, 2021

Cardinal Mar George Alencherry officially inaugurated the Year of Action in the presence of His Holiness the Blessed Bishops, Mar George Madathikandathil and Mar Chacko Thottumarickal.

On this occasion, Mar George Alencherry launched a digital magazine covering the diocesan activities.

Mar George Madathikandathil inaugurated the conference and Mar Chacko Thottumarickal delivered the blessing speech.

The meeting was attended by 14 office bearers and Sakha members

Feast of St. Mariam Thresia Chiramel  June 8, 2021

On the occasion of the feast of St. Mary Thresia, the mediator of the families, a poster containing the words of holiness was presented to the CML group and the children were asked to pray for the intercession of holiness.
